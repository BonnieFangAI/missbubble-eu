About

Miss Bubble AB is a Stockholm-based group working across lifestyle fashion, digital services, and cross-border business advisory.

Founded with a cross-cultural perspective spanning Asia and Northern Europe, we build brands and solutions that bring a little more beauty, confidence, and clarity into everyday life.

Founder

A decade of building across China and Sweden taught me one thing: the most meaningful work happens where worlds overlap — different cultures, different industries, different ways of seeing.

We're not here to tell grand stories. We're here to show up, build something real, and leave the world a little better than we found it. That's what Miss Bubble AB is — a small, stubborn bet that good work still matters.

Bonnie Fang — Founder & CEO
